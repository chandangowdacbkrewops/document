# 03. Product Goals & Success Metrics

---

# 1. Introduction

This document defines the strategic goals, measurable objectives, and key success metrics for the KrewOps Recruitment Platform. It establishes how product success will be evaluated throughout the product lifecycle and provides measurable indicators for product management, engineering, customer success, and executive leadership.

The goals and metrics described in this document align with the Business Requirements Document (BRD) and provide a framework for continuous improvement and product optimization.

# 5. North Star Metric

## Primary Success Metric

**Average Time-to-Hire**

Definition:

The average number of calendar days between job publication and candidate offer acceptance.

Business Objective:

Reduce average hiring time while maintaining or improving hiring quality.

Formula:

```
Average Time-to-Hire =
Total Hiring Days
----------------------------
Total Hires
```

# 6. Objectives and Key Results (OKRs)

## Objective 1

Improve recruitment efficiency.

### Key Results

- Reduce average hiring time by 30%.
- Reduce recruiter manual work by 40%.
- Automate at least 70% of repetitive recruitment activities.
- Increase recruiter productivity by 25%.

---

## Objective 2

Improve candidate experience.

### Key Results

- Candidate satisfaction score ≥ 4.5/5.
- Reduce application completion time.
- Improve communication response times.
- Reduce candidate drop-off rate.

---

## Objective 3

Improve hiring quality.

### Key Results

- Increase interview-to-offer conversion.
- Improve offer acceptance rate.
- Reduce poor hiring decisions.
- Increase hiring manager satisfaction.

---

## Objective 4

Increase platform adoption.

### Key Results

- Increase monthly active recruiters.
- Increase hiring manager participation.
- Increase self-service adoption.
- Increase workflow completion rate.
