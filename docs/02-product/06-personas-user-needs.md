# 06. Personas & User Needs

---

# 1. Introduction

This document defines the primary product personas for the KrewOps Recruitment Platform and captures their goals, responsibilities, challenges, workflows, and expectations.

Unlike the Business Requirements Document (BRD), which identifies stakeholders from a business perspective, this chapter focuses on how users interact with the product and what functionality is required to support their daily activities.

These personas guide product design, user experience, feature prioritization, and acceptance criteria.

---

# 2. Purpose

The objectives of this document are to:

- Identify primary product users.
- Understand user goals and motivations.
- Define user needs.
- Capture common pain points.
- Support UX design.
- Drive feature prioritization.
- Improve usability.
- Guide AI-assisted functionality.

---

|----------|------------------------|

# 4. Recruiter Persona

## Overview

Recruiters manage the complete hiring lifecycle from creating job requisitions to onboarding successful candidates.

### Responsibilities

- Create and publish jobs
- Source candidates
- Screen applications
- Schedule interviews
- Coordinate with hiring managers
- Manage offers
- Maintain candidate communication
- Track recruitment KPIs

### Goals

- Fill positions quickly.
- Improve candidate quality.
- Reduce manual work.
- Maintain organized recruitment pipelines.
- Improve hiring manager collaboration.

### Pain Points

- Manual resume screening.
- Scheduling conflicts.
- Poor candidate communication.
- Duplicate candidate profiles.
- Difficulty identifying suitable candidates.
- Repetitive administrative work.

### Product Needs

- Fast job creation.
- AI resume parsing.
- Candidate ranking.
- Automated notifications.
- Interview scheduling.
- Search and filtering.
- Workflow automation.
- Analytics dashboard.

### AI Assistance

- Resume summarization.
- Candidate recommendations.
- Interview question suggestions.
- Candidate-job matching.
- Candidate scoring.

### Success Metrics

- Reduced time-to-hire.
- Reduced manual effort.
- Increased placements.
- Higher recruiter productivity.
- Improved candidate satisfaction.

# 14. UX Design Principles

The product experience should prioritize:

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Progressive disclosure of information.
- Consistent navigation.
- Minimal clicks for common tasks.
- Clear feedback for user actions.
- Mobile-responsive layouts.
- Accessibility by design.
- Intelligent automation where it reduces manual effort.

# 15. Summary

This document defines the product personas and their specific needs within the KrewOps Recruitment Platform. By understanding each user's goals, responsibilities, challenges, and expected interactions, the product team can design features and experiences that improve usability, increase productivity, and deliver measurable business value. These personas provide the foundation for the Feature Catalog, Epics, User Stories, and Acceptance Criteria that follow in the Product Requirements Document.
