## Purpose

This document defines the business vision, objectives, scope, stakeholders, business rules, and requirements for the KrewOps workforce marketplace. It serves as the authoritative business document from which the PRD, SRS, Architecture, Database, API, Mobile, Backend, QA, and DevOps documentation will be derived.
