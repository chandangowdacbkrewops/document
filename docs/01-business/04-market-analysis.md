# Chapter 4 – Market Analysis

## Industry Overview

India has a large workforce engaged in agriculture, construction, logistics, manufacturing, hospitality, retail and home services. Temporary hiring is still dominated by offline networks and brokers.

## Current Challenges

- Slow hiring
- Dependence on personal contacts
- Poor pricing transparency
- Limited worker visibility
- No structured agreements
- Fragmented communication

## Opportunity

KrewOps digitizes the workforce marketplace by providing:

- Verified profiles
- Centralized search
- Dynamic work requests
- Proposal comparison
- Negotiation workflows
- Digital agreements
- Booking advances
- Ratings and reviews

## Target Sectors

- Agriculture
- Construction
- Home Services
- Manufacturing
- Logistics
- Hospitality
- Retail
- Skilled Trades

## Expected Outcomes

Reduced hiring time, increased worker visibility, improved transparency, repeat engagements and a trusted workforce ecosystem.
