# Purpose

This document defines the detailed product requirements for the KrewOps Recruitment Platform. It translates the approved Business Requirements Document (BRD) into implementation-ready specifications for product management, engineering, quality assurance, UX design, and operations.

The Product Requirements Document serves as the primary source of truth for feature behavior, user interactions, workflows, acceptance criteria, release planning, and product configuration.

---

# Scope

This document covers all functional areas of the KrewOps platform, including but not limited to:

- Multi-Tenant Organization Management
- Authentication & Authorization
- User & Role Management
- Job Management
- Candidate Management
- Recruitment Workflow
- Interview Management
- Offer Management
- Onboarding
- Notifications
- Document Management
- Search & Filtering
- Reporting & Analytics
- AI Capabilities
- Integrations
- Product Configuration
- Security & Compliance
- Administration

---

# Objectives

The objectives of this Product Requirements Document are to:

- Define detailed product behavior.
- Establish a common understanding between business and engineering teams.
- Provide implementation-ready requirements.
- Enable sprint planning and effort estimation.
- Support UX and interface design.
- Establish measurable acceptance criteria.
- Ensure traceability to business requirements.
- Reduce ambiguity during implementation.
- Improve product quality through standardized specifications.

# Traceability

Every product requirement shall trace back to one or more approved business requirements.

Likewise, every implemented feature shall trace back to:

- Product Goal
- Business Requirement
- User Story
- Acceptance Criteria
- Test Case
- Release

This ensures complete lifecycle traceability from concept through production.

---

# Assumptions

The Product Requirements Document assumes:

- Business Requirements have been approved.
- Stakeholders are available for clarification.
- Product roadmap has executive sponsorship.
- Technical architecture supports proposed capabilities.
- Development follows an Agile delivery model.

---

# Document Maintenance

This document shall be maintained throughout the product lifecycle.

Updates shall occur when:

- New features are introduced.
- Existing functionality changes.
- Business priorities shift.
- Customer feedback requires enhancements.
- Regulatory requirements change.
- Major releases are planned.

Document updates shall follow the established governance process.

---

# Summary

This Document Control chapter establishes the governance framework for the Product Requirements Document. It defines ownership, version control, approval workflows, traceability, and maintenance practices, ensuring that the PRD remains an authoritative, accurate, and auditable source of product requirements throughout the lifecycle of the KrewOps Recruitment Platform.
